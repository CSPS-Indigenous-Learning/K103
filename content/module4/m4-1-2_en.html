Self-reflection: Can you imagine paying $9.00 for two litres of milk? Or $14.00 for orange juice? How do you think these costs impact a family’s ability to provide for themselves?

Inuit believe that their traditional diet has many benefits over Western food. Yet food insecurity remains a significant risk for Inuit today. Nutrition North Canada is a Government of Canada program that helps provide Northerners in isolated communities with improved access to perishable nutritious food.
