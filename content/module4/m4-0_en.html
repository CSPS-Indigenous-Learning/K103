Chapter 4: Moving forward

Current socio-economic conditions

Inuit have made significant political gains. Some Inuit have access to Northern employment opportunities in sectors such as tourism, gas, mineral exploration and mining, and fisheries. Even so, Inuit still face many socio-economic challenges.

Accuracy of data

It is important to be aware that not all Indigenous people participate in the Canadian Census. Some Indigenous people choose not to because they don’t view Canada as their country. Some live in isolated communities and have limited access to internet and other means of communication. These factors have an impact on the accuracy of the data.
