Urban Centres

Increasingly, the Inuit population is rising in urban centres. About 27% of Inuit (or 18,000 Inuk) live outside of Inuit Nunangat. Based on the 2016 Census, the urban centres with the largest Inuit populations are Ottawa-Gatineau, Edmonton, Montréal, St. John’s and Toronto.

Did you know?

 Inuit reside in Chukotka (Russia), Greenland, Alaska and Canada, with a population of approximately 150,636 people.
