Question of

True or False?
Indigenous peoples of the Arctic are called Inuit, the singular form is Inuk.

 

Answer: True

Inuit are the Indigenous peoples of the Arctic. The word Inuit means “the people”.

Inuit Nunangat means "the place where Inuit live". How many regions make up Inuit Nunangat?

 

Answer: 4

Inuit live primarily in four regions of the Canadian Arctic, known as “Inuit Nunangat.”

These regions are:

  1.  Nunavut
  2. Nunavik
  3. Nunatsiavut
  4. Inuvialuit Settlement Region
Inuktitut is the language spoken by Inuit in Canada.

 

Answer: True

This linguistic term encompasses all Inuit language groups and dialects spoken in Inuit Nunangat.
