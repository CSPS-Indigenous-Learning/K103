Conclusion

The life challenges of Inuit communities are unique. Inuit have proven to be strong, resilient and determined in facing these challenges.

Here are a few examples of Inuit perseverance that have made a difference.

  • Pangnirtung is one of three Nunavut communities with a fish processing plant specializing in Arctic char and turbot
  • Atanarjuat: The Fast Runner by Zacharias Kunuk was voted the best Canadian film of all time
  • Jordin Tootoo is the first player in the National Hockey League of Inuk descent
  • Air Inuit is an Inuit-led airline company serving northern destinations
  • Qulliq Energy Corporation is 100% owned by the Government of Nunavut and supplies electricity to Nunavut communities

The Inuit population is young and vibrant with incredible resilience. This energy will propel Inuit as they work to preserve their culture and increase their standard of living for future generations.

The North is a place that many of us still have to discover. Inuit are growing their tourist industry — when do you plan to explore beautiful Nunangat?
