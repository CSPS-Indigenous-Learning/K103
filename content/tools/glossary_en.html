Glossary

  • Mother tongue

    Mother tongue refers to the first language learned at home in childhood and that is still understood by the individual at the time of the census.

  • Tuberculosis

    Tuberculosis (TB) is a disease caused by bacteria called Mycobacterium tuberculosis. The bacteria usually attack the lungs, but can also damage other parts of the body. While curable today, if left untreated TB can be fatal.

  • Food Insecurity

    Food insecurity exists when someone does not have physical and economic access to sufficient, safe and nutritious food. This can range from not having the ability to afford a balanced diet, to not being able to eat culturally acceptable foods, to missing meals or not eating for days at a time.
