Introduction

Inuit are the traditional knowledge keepers of the Arctic lands in Canada. They have strong community values such as sharing, respect and cooperation. Their leaders, musicians and artists have advanced Inuit self-determination and human rights.

This course provides a brief introduction to Inuit in Canada. You will learn a bit about their traditions, culture, governance structures and history. The course will also help you better understand the relationship between Inuit and the Government of Canada.

Throughout the course, you will be invited to self-reflect on some important questions. We invite you to download this learning journal to capture your thoughts.
